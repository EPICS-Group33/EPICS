FAQ

Common Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we get :

What is Risk Profiling?

Patient risk profiling is a process of assessing the likelihood of a patient developing certain health conditions or experiencing adverse events based on various factors such as medical history, lifestyle, and genetic predisposition.

How is pateient risk profiling beneficial?

Patient risk profiling can help healthcare providers identify high-risk patients early on, allowing for targeted interventions and personalized treatment plans to prevent or manage potential health issues more effectively.

Is risk profiling accurate?

Patient risk profiling utilizes advanced algorithms and data analysis techniques to provide insights into an individual's health risks. While it can offer valuable information, it is important to remember that no prediction method is 100% accurate.

How can I access my patient risk profile results?

Once you have completed the patient risk profiling assessment, you can access your results through your user dashboard on our website, desktop or android app. Your profile will include personalized recommendations and insights based on the assessment.

Can patient risk profiling be used for preventive healthcare?

Yes, patient risk profiling is often used in preventive healthcare to identify individuals at higher risk for certain conditions and provide proactive measures to reduce those risks. By addressing potential health issues early, preventive strategies can lead to better health outcomes.
